It is important to keep the syntax as it is now:	
" Partition header definition should be kept as x.x.x, followed by space, {N|H|A\W}, space, hyphen-minus, space, ""partition name"". The first entry should be x.x.1."	
" Ranges are expressed either as single 4 hex value followed by tab, or as a pair of 4 hex values separated by a hyphen-minus and followed by a tab. The character name part is only for easy reference and is not used by the parser."
