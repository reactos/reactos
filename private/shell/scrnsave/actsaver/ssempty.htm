Microsoft Internet Explorer Screen Saver Setup
  The Microsoft IE4 screen saver not only protects your monitor, but brings your favorite sites from the world wide web right to your desktop! Follow the instructions below to identify the sites you would like the screen saver to display. The screen saver will do the rest.
  • Start the IE4 browser
  • Go to the site you would like to add to the screen saver
  • Add the site to your Favorites and check 'Subscribe to Site'
  • Do this for as many sites as you wish
  • The next time the screen saver activates, you will see these sites displayed.
